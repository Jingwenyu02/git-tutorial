Feature extraction
- Create 128 byte representation of the face
Classification
- Match encoding to faces in my database

Face recognition is the process of identifying an individual's face through images or videos. There are color pixels and grayscale pixels. Color pixels have red, green, and blue channels. These three colors creates colors of different combination in different amounts. On the other hand, grayscale only have a single channel that has a range from white to black, therefore, they are simpler to work with.
